Fruit and yogurt smoothie recipe

Description

A tasty fruit and yogurt smoothie is one of the better options for health. This flavorful drink is the right amount of sweet while making sure you get the nutrients needed to keep you going. This easy to follow recipe takes a certain amount of each ingredient and mixes it in the blender to create the tasty goodness.

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

    Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
